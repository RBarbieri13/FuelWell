# Deep-research reports

Eight reports are being generated in parallel to inform FuelWell's product, design, and go-to-market strategy. They land here as they complete:

1. `01-market-landscape-and-competitors.md` — who's in the market, user counts, recent funding.
2. `02-consumer-pain-points-and-unmet-needs.md` — what real users complain about on App Store, Reddit, forums.
3. `03-pricing-and-monetization-models.md` — what competitors actually charge, plus a pricing recommendation.
4. `04-feature-comparison-matrix.md` — 22 apps × ~32 features, with table-stakes vs differentiator analysis.
5. `05-ux-ui-patterns-and-design-best-practices.md` — onboarding, logging, paywall, progress-viz patterns worth copying.
6. `06-emerging-trends-and-market-gaps.md` — GLP-1, AI-native apps, wearables convergence, longevity, women's health.
7. `07-target-audience-segmentation-and-personas.md` — who the beachhead users are.
8. `08-strategic-positioning-and-differentiation-for-fuelwell.md` — synthesis + the single biggest strategic call.

Pair these with the founder-gut brief in `research/inspiration/INSPIRATION_TEMPLATE.md` when handing off to the app-building agent.
