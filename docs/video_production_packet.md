# FuelWell Product Video — Production Packet

## Executive Summary

This packet contains everything needed to produce a 60-second hero product video (plus 30s and 15s short cuts) for FuelWell's launch. The video's central thesis: **"FuelWell doesn't just track — it thinks for you."** The AI Coach is the hero. Everything else supports it.

---

## 1. Creative Direction Overview

### The Big Idea
**"You don't need another calorie counter. You need a coach."**

This single sentence drives every creative decision. It positions FuelWell in a category of ONE — separating it from every tracker, counter, and logging app on the market.

### Emotional Arc (60 seconds)
| Timestamp | Emotion | What Viewer Feels |
|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| 0–3s | Seen / Frustrated | "That's literally me" |
| 3–10s | Validated | "Finally someone gets it" |
| 10–15s | Curious | "Wait, what is this?" |
| 15–35s | Impressed | "That's actually smart" |
| 35–50s | Trusting | "This seems real and legit" |
| 50–60s | Empowered | "I can do this — downloading now" |

### Visual Style
- **Primary:** Clean & minimal (Apple-style) — matches the app's design system
- **Secondary:** Warm & human for lifestyle B-roll — real kitchens, real light, real people
- **Accent:** Subtle AI-forward glow ONLY during the Coach chat moment
- **Color Grade:** Warm, slightly desaturated, consistent across all footage

### Brand Palette (match exactly in all assets)
| Token | Hex | Use |
|-------|-----|-----|
| Primary | #22c55e | Calorie ring, CTA buttons, logo "Fuel" |
| Accent | #f97316 | Secondary actions, photo mode bg |
| Neutral-900 | #171717 | Text, logo "Well" |
| Protein | #3b82f6 | Macro bar (blue) |
| Carbs | #f59e0b | Macro bar (amber) |
| Fat | #ef4444 | Macro bar (red) |
| Background | #fafafa | App backgrounds |

### Typography
- **Font:** Geist (sans-serif) — used throughout app and all text overlays
- **Data:** tabular-nums for any numbers on screen
- **Caption Font:** Geist, white with subtle dark shadow

## 2. Script — 60-Second Hero Cut

### HOOK (0–3s)
**[VISUAL]** Close-up of a phone screen showing a cluttered home screen with 6+ fitness app icons. A thumb swipes to delete one. Then another.

**[VO]** "You've downloaded 6 fitness apps this year. Deleted 5."

**[SOUND]** Soft "delete" swoosh × 2

---

### PAIN POINT (3–10s)
**[VISUAL]** Quick montage (3 shots, 2s each):
1. Someone squinting at a confusing macro spreadsheet on their phone
2. A generic meal plan with boring food photos (stock app screenshot)
3. A finger hovering over an "Are you sure you want to delete?" dialog

**[VO]** "Generic plans. Confusing macros. Apps that feel like homework."

**[SOUND]** Low, slightly tense ambient music building underneath

---

### THE TURN — FuelWell Reveal (10–15s)
**[VISUAL]** Smooth transition — the cluttered screen morphs into FuelWell's clean marketing landing page. Logo animates in. Then the screen transitions to the onboarding wizard (Step 9 — the reveal).

**[VO]** "Meet FuelWell. AI that actually learns how you eat, move, and live."

**[SOUND]** Music lifts — warm electronic build. Subtle "reveal" chime.

---

### FEATURE SHOWCASE — Proof Beats (15–40s)

#### Beat 1: Personalization (15–20s)
**[VISUAL]** Onboarding Step 9 — the personalized targets appear. "2,100 kcal/day" animates in large. Three macro cards slide in: Protein 150g (blue), Carbs 263g (amber), Fat 58g (red). Goal: "Lose Weight." Diet: "Vegetarian."

**[VO]** "Your goals. Your diet. Your allergies. Your plan — calculated in seconds."

**[SOUND]** Soft UI "pop" as each card appears

#### Beat 2: AI Coach (20–30s) — THE HERO MOMENT
**[VISUAL]** Cut to the AI Coach chat screen. User types: "I'm at a Mexican restaurant — what should I order?" Typing indicator appears (three dots, staggered). AI responds: "Skip the chips and queso — try the grilled fish tacos with extra veggies. You'll hit about 580 cal and 35g protein. Want me to adjust your dinner plan?"

Camera LINGERS here. This is the ad.

**[VO]** "Ask your AI coach anything. It knows your macros, your restrictions, and exactly what to suggest."

**[SOUND]** Soft "message received" chime. Music peaks during this section.

**[INTERCUT]** Brief 1.5s lifestyle shot — person at a restaurant glancing at phone with a smile.

#### Beat 3: Dashboard + Speed (30–37s)
**[VISUAL]** Dashboard — calorie ring animates from 0 to 1,247/2,100. Green ring fills smoothly. Three macro bars animate in sequence. Then QUICK CUT to meal logging — user taps "Log Meal," types "chicken," taps plus on "Grilled Chicken Breast" — done in 4 seconds.

**[VO]** "See your day at a glance. Log meals in seconds, not minutes."

**[SOUND]** Quick tap sounds for each UI interaction. Satisfying "fill" sound as the calorie ring animates.

#### Beat 4: Lifestyle Montage (37–42s)
**[VISUAL]** Rapid lifestyle B-roll montage (5 shots, 1s each):
1. Phone on kitchen counter showing meal plan, person chopping vegetables behind
2. Morning walk, person glances at phone showing dashboard
3. Adaptive athlete (wheelchair) checking phone between training sets
4. Person at desk, quick glance at phone showing calorie summary
5. Grocery store aisle, person checking phone

**[VO]** "FuelWell fits your life — wherever you are."

**[SOUND]** Music maintains energy through the montage

---

### TRUST BEAT (42–48s)
**[VISUAL]** Brief flash of the Progress screen mockup — weight trend line going down gently, calorie adherence heatmap mostly green, "8-day streak" badge. Then a text overlay: "Built by two people who got tired of apps that felt like homework."

**[VO]** "Real coaching. Real progress. Built for real life."

**[SOUND]** Music resolves to warm, confident tone

---

### CTA — Close (48–60s)
**[VISUAL]** FuelWell logo lockup — "Fuel" in green, "Well" in dark — centered. App store badges (Apple + Google Play) appear below. QR code fades in on the right. Final tagline appears: "Eat smarter. Feel better."

**[VO]** "Download FuelWell. Your free trial starts today."

**[SOUND]** Music resolves. Final note holds. Silence after VO for the logo to breathe.

## 5. Asset Production Requirements

### A. App UI Screen Captures (Card 3.1)

**From Real App (screen record):**
| Screen | What to Capture | Duration |
|--------|----------------|----------|
| Onboarding Step 9 | Targets animating in: calorie number + 3 macro cards | 5s |
| Dashboard | Calorie ring filling + macro bars animating | 5s |
| AI Coach Chat | Full conversation: question → typing indicator → response | 10s |
| Meal Log | Search "chicken" → tap plus on result | 4s |
| Marketing Landing Page | Hero section with logo reveal | 2s |

**Generated Mockups (must match design system exactly):**
| Screen | Purpose | Specifications |
|--------|---------|----------------|
| Progress Dashboard (populated) | Show what tracking looks like after 12 days | Weight line chart trending down, calorie heatmap (green/yellow), 8-day streak badge, protein consistency 85% |
| Meal Plan (weekly view) | Flash of "what's coming" | 7-day grid with meal thumbnails, macro totals per day |
| Recipe Detail | Brief flash during montage | Recipe card with photo, macros, prep time, ingredients list |

**Mockup Design Requirements:**
- Use exact FuelWell design tokens (colors, spacing, fonts from `design-tokens.ts`)
- Same component patterns: Card (elevated variant), Badge, rounded corners (0.75rem)
- Same background color (#fafafa), same header patterns
- iPhone 15 Pro frame (393×852 viewport)
- Must be indistinguishable from built screens

### B. AI-Generated Lifestyle B-Roll (Card 3.2)

**Tool:** Runway Gen-3 Alpha (primary), Kling (backup for longer clips)

| Shot | Prompt Direction | Duration | Priority |
|------|-----------------|----------|----------|
| Kitchen meal prep | Person (30s, diverse) chopping vegetables at a bright kitchen counter, phone propped up showing a green-themed app, warm natural light from window, shallow DOF, cinematic 4K | 4s | P0 |
| Restaurant check | Person at a casual restaurant table, glancing at phone and smiling, ambient restaurant background, warm lighting, medium shot | 3s | P0 |
| Morning walk | Person walking outdoors in morning light, casually glancing at phone, athletic wear, natural setting, tracking shot | 2s | P1 |
| Adaptive athlete | Wheelchair athlete in a gym/training environment, checking phone between sets, confident expression, well-lit, respectful framing | 3s | P1 |
| Desk/office check | Person at a modern desk, quick glance at phone, professional setting, bright and clean | 2s | P1 |
| Grocery store | Person in a grocery aisle holding phone in one hand, produce section, natural supermarket lighting | 2s | P1 |

**Prompt Engineering Rules:**
- Always specify: "cinematic, shallow depth of field, warm color grade, 4K, natural motion"
- Diverse cast: vary age (25-55), ethnicity, gender, body type across shots
- Avoid: uncanny hands, unnatural facial expressions, overly staged poses
- Generate 3 variations per shot, select the most natural
- Color grade all final selects to match FuelWell's warm-green palette

### C. Voiceover (Card 3.3)

**Tool:** ElevenLabs (Eleven Multilingual v2 model)

**Voice Requirements:**
- Gender-neutral or female — warm, mid-range pitch
- Conversational pacing — sounds like a friend, not a presenter
- "Slight smile" in delivery — friendly energy without being bubbly
- NO announcer cadence, NO whispery ASMR, NO uptalking

**Files to Generate:**
1. Full 60s script — 3 voice options
2. Each alternate hook (A, B, C) — all 3 voices
3. 30s cut script — best voice
4. 15s cut script — best voice
5. Export: WAV (lossless) + MP3 (preview)
6. Timing sheet with exact timestamps per line

### D. Music & Sound Design (Card 3.4)

**Track Requirements:**
- Genre: Upbeat electronic / lo-fi motivational
- BPM: 120–130
- Structure: Soft intro (hook) → building (pain point) → lift (reveal) → peak energy (feature showcase) → warm resolve (CTA)
- Duration: 65s (with 5s tail for editing flexibility)
- Royalty-free, cleared for commercial use including paid ads

**Source Options (ranked):**
1. Epidemic Sound — best selection for this style, $15/month
2. Artlist — alternative, $10/month
3. Suno AI — generate custom track to spec if libraries don't have the right feel

**Sound Effects Needed:**
| SFX | When | Source |
|-----|------|--------|
| App delete swoosh | Hook (0–3s) | Freesound.org or Epidemic Sound |
| UI tap/click (subtle) | Every app interaction | Record from real iPhone or use Epidemic Sound |
| Card appear "pop" | Onboarding reveal | Subtle, not cartoonish |
| Message received chime | AI Coach response appears | Soft, premium — like iMessage but unique |
| Calorie ring fill | Dashboard animation | Smooth rising tone, 1s |
| Achievement chime | Streak badge appears | Warm, celebratory but brief |
| Transition whoosh | Between sections | Subtle, not dramatic |

**Audio Mix Guide:**
- Voiceover: -6dB (always dominant)
- Music: -18dB (bed, never competes with VO)
- SFX: -12dB (noticeable but not distracting)

4. Record screen interactions using macOS built-in screen recording or OBS
5. Alternatively: screen record on a real iPhone 15/16 Pro running the app
